About Me

I’m Zishan. I grew up in Dubai, did my undergrad in Bangalore, worked as an accountant and an e‑commerce data analyst back in Dubai, completed my master’s in Business Analytics at UEA, and now I somehow teach teenagers in London. Life really took the scenic route with me.

I’ve done a bit of everything: balancing books, analysing sales, building dashboards, and now explaining photosynthesis to Year 8s on a random Tuesday morning. Somewhere along the way I realised I actually enjoy the mix of data, learning, and people. That combination turned into an interest in EdTech before I even realised what EdTech was.

These days I build practical analytics projects, support students in the classroom, and try to make learning a little clearer and a little less painful for everyone involved. I like things that are simple, useful, and actually help someone, whether it’s a dashboard or a lesson.

If you’re into education, data, or just appreciate a good career plot twist, we’re already aligned.
